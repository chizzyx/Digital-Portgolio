Soundboard

Identity:

Soundboard allows you to combine 2 or more beats/instrumentals to create your own unique melody. With Soundboard you can take a piece from mozart and combine it with song a from 21 savage. Despite their music being polar opposites you can take the two to create a unique sound unlike any other.

Problem:

Isolating audios and vocals to combine can be a very time consuming process.

Solution:

Soundboard does the work of isolating the audios as well as combining them so you can hear your custom beats sooner without the heavy workload.

Target Market:

The Target Market for Soundboard is anyone who enjoys music. Whether that be listeners or sound engineers.

The Competition:

There are platflorms that allow you to crossfade music so they flow into one another but not any that I can find that allows you to layer music on top of each other.

Revenue streams:

Soundboard will sell to customers through a membership fee. To be allowed access to the app/website you will need to pay 2.99 a onth.

Marketing Activities:

Soundboard will advertise to customers through targeted google and social media ads. Spotify has a tracking app called spotistats which allows you to track your listening habits. Soundboard could integrate itself into the app and allow you to hear what your favorite songs combined would sound like.

Expenses:

We will need to pay for the targeted ads and a space on spotistats.
